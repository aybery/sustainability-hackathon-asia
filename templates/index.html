Welcome to the Sustainability Hackathon Asia!

The focus of this project is to predict the spread of Cholera in Myanmar due to flooding.

Our goal for this hackathon was to reliably predict the risk of cholera outbreaks from data such as the likeliness and levels of flooding in a given area in Myanmar, and from this data give a 'Vaccine Priority Score' representing the necessity and urgency of immunisation outreach to the area.
