Flood Risk Monitoring

We analyse rainfall data, river levels, and historical flood records across all 15 states and regions of Myanmar to score flood risk on a scale of 1–10.

🦠

Cholera Outbreak Prediction

Flooding contaminates water supplies and sanitation systems, dramatically increasing cholera risk. Our model predicts outbreak probability using flood severity and population density.

💉

Vaccine Priority Score

Each region receives a Vaccine Priority Score (0–100) combining flood risk, cholera likelihood, and affected population size, guiding immunisation outreach efforts.

How It Works

  1. 1 Flood severity data is collected for each region from satellite and ground sensors.
  2. 2 A cholera risk score is computed based on flood level, water quality proxies, and population density.
  3. 3 A combined Vaccine Priority Score guides health authorities on where to deploy vaccines first.
  4. 4 The interactive map displays all regions colour-coded by urgency — red (HIGH), amber (MEDIUM), green (LOW).
