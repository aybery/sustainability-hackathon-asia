Insufficient vaccine coverage during flood events has repeatedly allowed cholera to spread unchecked in Myanmar's most vulnerable regions. The Ayeyarwady Delta, Rakhine Coast, and Mon State have seen the highest burden.

Our system calculates the minimum vaccine doses needed per region based on at-risk population, flood probability, and past outbreak frequency.
